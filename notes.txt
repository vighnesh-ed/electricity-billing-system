Reponsive webpage for electricity billing in XAMPP and PHP with a form having the following fields
Month Selection Dropdown, Name, Address(bigger input box), Mobile number, number of units, submit button

Rates:
50 units -> 3.50Rs/units
100 units -> 4.00Rs/units
250 units -> 5.20Rs/units
Beyond 250 units-> 6.50Rs/units

Rates are additive and not multiplicative. Eg if 150 units, first 50 will have 3.50 then the rest will have 4.00.
